About The Hiit Crew

The Hiit Crew combines functional body weight movements, exercise machines, boxing, core, stabilization and strength into a circuit that will get your heart rate pumping. Our workouts are fast, furious and fun in a team training environment. Workout with like minded people who will challenge and support you through your journey. Our mission is to create an experience that is challenging, enjoyable, rewarding and ultimately an environment where you can transform yourself into a better version of you. You will leave feeling better about yourself!

Hiit Crew Classes

  • Cardio
    Cardio at the Hiit Crew happens on monday, wednesday and friday. With monday and fridays including boxing. with classes such as 3 peat, amraps, double trouble, warrior plus, triple drop and much more.
  • Strength
    Strength at the Hiit Crew happens on tuesday, thursday. Strength classes can change from normal specfic strength, specfic stregth volume and stregth testing
  • Crossfit
    Crossfit classes are held on wednesday at 6:20pm and friday 5:20pm as well as saturday 9:20am for comp training these classes will have movments such as t2b, double unders, DB snatch, over head squats, barbell snatch and split jerk as well as other commun crossfit movments. All these skills will be taught just like in a normal class.

Dan
